Asset Management App

Finances • Stocks • Real Estate • Employees

01/ Overview

Developed a hybrid asset management application and web-based management tool (iOS/Android) using React and React Native for the largest retail conglomerate in Brazil.

As the sole frontend/mobile developer, I collaborated closely with backend developers and the product team. I also provided significant input into product design, assisting the UX team with technical knowledge to contribute to the conception of user experiences.

03/ Delivery

Even though the project was complex and we were a small team, we were able to deliver the project on time and within budget. The application was well received by the client and the end-users, and it was a significant success for the company having a large number of daily active users.
